Legends of The Masters

The Legacy of Three Masters Champions

Posted by ABG-SI on January 2024

Jack Nicklaus

With 18 professional championship wins, Jack Nicklaus goes down as one of the greatest golfers to have ever played the game. Six of these wins came from competing at the Masters. Years 1963, 1965, 1966, 1972, 1975, and 1986.

Tiger Woods

For every sport, there is always "one" that sticks out from all the rest. Someone who is responsible for defining the sport, as well as changing the game. The man responsible for changing the game of Golf, is Tiger Woods. Tiger has won 15 majors, five of which are from competing at the Masters. Years 1997, 2001, 2002, 2005, and 2019.

Arnold Palmer

Nicknamed "The King", Palmer is known for being the trailblazer for the game of Golf. Beginning in the 1950s, Arnold Palmer was one of the first greatest television superstars. Winning seven major championships, four of these championships came from competing at the Masters. Years 1958, 1960, 1962, and 1964.
